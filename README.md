# 📊 HR Analytics: Employee Attrition Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes HR employee data to identify key factors influencing employee attrition, job satisfaction, and workforce distribution. The goal is to generate actionable insights that can help organizations improve employee retention and optimize HR strategies.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Employee attrition is a major challenge for organizations as it leads to increased hiring costs and productivity loss. This project aims to:

* Identify factors contributing to employee attrition
* Analyze employee demographics and job roles
* Provide data-driven recommendations to improve retention

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn

---

## 📁 Dataset Information

* **Total Records:** 1470 employees
* **Total Features:** 35 columns

### Key Features:

* Demographics: Age, Gender, Marital Status
* Job Details: Department, Job Role, Job Level
* Compensation: Monthly Income, Salary Hike
* Work Metrics: Work-Life Balance, Overtime, Years at Company
* Target Variable: Attrition

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning & Preparation

* Verified dataset structure and dimensions
* Checked for missing values (no null values found)
* Removed duplicate records
* Ensured data consistency and accuracy

---

## 📊 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

### 🔹 Categorical Analysis

* Employee distribution by Department, Gender, and Job Role
* Analysis of Business Travel and Overtime
* Attrition comparison across Job Roles

### 🔹 Numerical Analysis

* Distribution of Monthly Income, Age, and Experience
* Years at Company and Total Working Years analysis

### 🔹 Visualization Techniques

* Count Plots
* Histograms
* Box Plots
* Attrition comparison charts

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Employees working **overtime** show significantly higher attrition
* Certain **job roles** experience higher employee turnover
* **Salary distribution** varies widely across roles
* Majority of employees belong to **Research & Development** department
* Employees with **lower job satisfaction** are more likely to leave

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Reduce excessive overtime workload to improve employee retention
* Focus on improving job satisfaction through engagement programs
* Provide competitive salary adjustments for high-risk job roles
* Implement targeted retention strategies for departments with high attrition

---

## 🚀 Project Outcome

The analysis provides valuable insights into workforce behavior and attrition patterns, enabling HR teams to make informed decisions and design effective employee retention strategies.
